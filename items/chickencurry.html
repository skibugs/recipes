Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken curry is a flavorful and aromatic dish made by cooking tender chicken pieces in a rich, spiced gravy. The curry is prepared using onions, tomatoes, garlic, ginger, and a blend of spices like turmeric, cumin, coriander, and garam masala. The chicken is simmered in this fragrant sauce until it absorbs all the flavors and becomes juicy. Coconut milk or yogurt can be added for extra creaminess. Chicken curry is best enjoyed with rice, naan, or roti, making it a comforting and delicious meal.

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup water
  • ½ cup yogurt or coconut milk (optional, for creaminess)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro (for garnish)

Steps:

  • Heat Oil: In a pan, heat oil over medium heat and sauté the chopped onions until golden brown.
  • Add Ginger-Garlic Paste: Stir in the ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute until fragrant.
  • Add Tomatoes and Spices: Add the pureed tomatoes, turmeric, cumin, coriander, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  • Add Chicken: Add the chicken pieces and mix well to coat them with the spices.
  • Simmer: Pour in water, cover, and let it simmer for 20-25 minutes until the chicken is tender.
  • Add Yogurt or Coconut Milk (Optional): Stir in yogurt or coconut milk for a creamy texture and let it cook for another 5 minutes.
  • Finish with Garam Masala: Sprinkle garam masala and mix well.
  • Garnish and Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot with rice, naan, or roti.
